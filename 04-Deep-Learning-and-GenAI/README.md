# DeepGen-Protein: Representation Learning and Generative Modeling of Protein Sequences
**Subject:** Fundamentals of Deep Learning and Generative AI Techniques

## Summary
A deep learning framework covering two tasks: (1) protein family classification using 1D-CNN, LSTM, and GRU architectures, all reaching over 99.7% accuracy on 20 protein families from the Pfam dataset, and (2) generative modeling of protein sequences using a Variational Autoencoder (VAE) and a Conditional GAN, trained on Pfam and UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot data. The VAE produced structured, smoothly-interpolating latent representations across protein families, while the cGAN generated diverse class-conditioned sequences, with both achieving 100% unique sequence generation.
