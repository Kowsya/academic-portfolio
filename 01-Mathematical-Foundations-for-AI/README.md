# Chi-Square Analysis of Student Performance and Lifestyle Factors
**Subject:** Mathematical Foundations for AI

## Summary
A statistical study examining whether lifestyle habits — screen time, sleep, and study hours — are related to academic performance. Data was collected from 39 students via a survey and analyzed in Python using the Chi-Square Test of Independence in a Colab notebook. None of the relationships reached statistical significance, though two were borderline, which pointed to the need for a larger sample and reinforced the value of a data-driven approach to studying student wellness.
